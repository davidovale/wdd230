Hick's Law

Amazon

amazon.com

Hick’s Law says that every additional choice increases the time required to make a decision. The more options a user has on your website, the more difficult it is to use (if it’s used at all). We need to eliminate choices. We can see that through the Search Filters. The user can eliminate choices using the filters.

Fitts's Law

Online Video Converter

onlinevideoconverter.com

Fitt’s law stipulates that the time required to move to a target area (e.g. click a button) is a function of the distance to the target and the size of the target. In other words, the bigger an object and the closer it is, the easier it is to use. OnlineVideoConverter makes it easier to hit “Start” than other links

White Space and Clean Design

MADE

made.com

White space is the part of a web page that remains “empty”. It is the space between graphics, margins, gutters, space between columns, space between lines of text or visuals. We can see this all over the made.com site
